Curiosity made me a developer

Hi, my name is Cora Marcu.

For a decade I built my career in marketing and project management, but things took an exciting new turn for me last year, when I decided to start learning how to code. I had been curious about programming for a while, but as a PM I also wanted to understand more about the dev team’s specific problems and needs.

I am proud to say that I have come a long way since then. Over the past year I have given myself a strong foundation in Java, JavaScript, HTML and CSS, and I have also fallen hopelessly in love with coding.

My creative side resonated the most with front end development, which brings us to where we are today.
